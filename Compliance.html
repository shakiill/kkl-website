Fire Protection

  • Industrial health, safety and welfare are closely monitored and worker friendly environment is ensured in all the facilities of Kaizer Knitwears ltd.
  • Preventive measures: ‘Safety first!’ is our slogan and we strongly believe it in our daily working life in Kaizer Knitwears Ltd. Fire is one of our primary and vital safety concern. We conduct unnoticed fire drills more than once in a month to improve the employees efficiency on evacuation in case of real fire incident.
  • During fire: A group of dedicated and efficient fire fighter at every individual units are always remain stand by to take part in extinguishing fire directly as and when require.
  • Fire procedure: It is also an integral part of fire drill once fire is managed, a small team will dig out whether there are any wounded persons remain in the floors, if found arrange taking them to the nearest hospital followed by initial first aid treatment.

Fire Detraction

  • Kaizer Knitwears Ltd. regards fire as a critical safety concern. To maintain a high level of preparedness, we conduct Regular unannounced fire Drills to enhance employees’ evacuation efficiency in the event of an actual fire. Our facility is equipped with a state-of-the-art fire protection system including Fire alarms, Smoke Detectors, Sprinklers, and other essential safety measures, ensuring the Highest Standard of Employee Safety , which remains our foremost responsibility.

In house Medical facilities

  • The welfare of the employees at Kaizer Knitwears Ltd. is an important concern to us that is why we have a team of professional doctors and well trained nurses at standby on the factory floors. The medical team stands ready with the basic first-aid kit and other necessary medicine. The staff undergoes regular checkups and the company pays for the consultation and the recommended medicine.

Child Care Centre

  • Our Child Care Centre offers a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment for children under six, designed especially for our female employees. With space for up to 20 children, the centre features dedicated sleeping, breastfeeding, and play zones, complete with toys and indoor games. We focus on both care and development—helping children grow, learn, and become independent in a warm, hygienic setting. On-site cleaning and drying facilities ensure cleanliness, while one caring female attendants provide attentive supervision at all times.

Dining room and Canteen facilities

  • A spacious dining room is present for the benefit of the employees where they take their lunch in clean and healthy environment. Every Working Day All Employee happly enjoy our canteen Facility..

Training & Development

  • Training is an educational process. People can learn new information, re-learn and reinforce existing knowledge and skills, and most importantly have time to think and consider what new options can help them improve their effectiveness at work. Effective trainings convey relevant and useful information that inform participants and develop skills and behaviors that can be transferred back to the workplace. Training & Development create a learning environment where employees’ will be prepared to accept change, develop new skills and take responsibility for their own continuous learning, in partnership with their Immediate Manager & Line Manager to ensure their effective contribution to the successful achievement of both business and personal goals.

Worker’s Participations Committee

  • Worker’s Participation Committee (WPC)- is an unique team building initiative in the garment industry. This committee helps to achieve objective by creating bridge between the top management & workers. Through this committee, workers and management uphold their mutual interest.
