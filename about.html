Our Story

Leading RMG Manufacturer
Since 2001

Kaizer Knitwears Ltd. started its journey in 2001. Today, it has become one of the top knit-based textile manufacturing powerhouses in Bangladesh — with a fully vertical setup covering every aspect of production, from knitting and dyeing to sewing, embroidery, and printing.

Recognized by renowned global brands including Bang Bang, Vegotex, Fox, Pepco, New Yorker and Switch, Kaizer Knitwears Ltd. maintains a dynamic supply chain capable of handling even the shortest lead times.
