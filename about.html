Our Story

Leading RMG Manufacturer Since 2001

Kaizer Knitwears Ltd started its journey in the year 2001. Today, Kaizer Knit wears Ltd has become one of the top knit-based textile manufacturing power houses in Bangladesh, with a fully vertical setup that covers every aspect of production, from knitting and dyeing to sewing, embroidery, and printing recognized by renowned brands such as Bang Bang, Vegotex, Fox, Pepco, New Yorker and Switch. Kaizer Knitwears Ltd is able to maintain a dynamic supply chain that can handle even the shortest lead times.

  • Name of Company: Kaizer Knitwears Ltd.
  • Year of Establishment: 2001
  • Legal Status: Private Limited Company
  • Type of Business: 100% Export Oriented Knit Composite Industry
  • E-Mail: info@kaizerknitwear.com
  • Member of: BGMEA (Bangladesh Garments Manufacturers & Exporters Association)

Green and Responsible

Committed to Sustainability

KAIZER is dedicated to sustainability, minimizing environmental impact through energy conservation, pollution prevention, and eco-friendly practices.

By using environment-friendly dyes, recycling waste, and reducing greenhouse gas emissions, KAIZER ensures responsible manufacturing.

KAIZER actively supports ILO regulations, child labor elimination, and community welfare, leveraging its 2,116+ skilled employees to drive positive change.

The company also prioritizes employee well-being through recreation programs and annual festivities.
